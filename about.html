Our Story

The Taste of Tradition

At Al Dimashqi Restaurant, we believe that food is not just sustenance, but an experience that transports you to a different time and place. Rooted in the rich culinary heritage of Damascus, our chefs expertly craft each dish using time-honored recipes passed down through generations.

From the fragrant spices imported directly from the Levant to the freshest local produce, every ingredient is chosen with absolute care. We don't just serve meals; we serve memories of family gatherings, bustling souks, and historic dining halls.

Join us on a culinary journey and experience the legendary hospitality of Syria right here in Abu Dhabi.
